London October the 8<sup>th</sup> 1706
Late last night arrived two mayles [mails]
from Holland which bring the following
newes [news] -- Vienna October the 2<sup>d</sup> -- the
Emperor has sent Prince Eugene a
sword vallued [valued] at 30000 Guilders. --
Milan September the 29<sup>th</sup> the Duke of Savoy
and Prince Eugene arrived here the 26<sup>th</sup>
and with them Count Baroma who
is made Gov<ex>ernor</ex> of this Citty [City]. - Venice
October the 3<sup>d</sup> - the Governor of
Nova intending to defend the place
against the Germans sent for some
with him but whilst these were
the Gates and let the Germans in
who made the Gov<ex>erno</ex>r and Garryson Garrison
