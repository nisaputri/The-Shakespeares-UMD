London July the 31 - 1705
Last satterday orders were given at
the admirallty office for sending
severall of our third and fourth rate
orders sent to Guernsey and Jersey
to send out their Privateers to doe
Torbay from the soundings in order
to take in fresh water and is in the
mean time the ffrench fleet
sayle out of Brest they are
prepared to follow them -
