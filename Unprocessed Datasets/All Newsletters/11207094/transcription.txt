London August 24<sup>th</sup> 1708
Lisbon August the 20th His Excellency
Mr. Methwyn some Dayes since
went on Board the Warspight
Commanded by Commodore Hicks
with Severall English Officers
who have got leave to be Absent
for three months. - The Marquess
of Fronteira being Returned
from making Excursions in
Andalusia holds daily Conferences
with the Duke of Cadavall and
Earle of Galway about Opening
the Campaign on the 3<sup>d</sup> of
September next and it is Resolved
If they cannot force the Spaniards
to a battle, the Seige of Badajos
is to be again Attempted -
