That the Duke of Savoy had left
some foot to Invade Dauphiney
he keeps his Troopes in good Discipline
St Laurence and another place
and the soldiers have pluckt Up
their Vines by the Rootes by
haveing burnt the Olive Trees
in the County of Nice - These
Letters confirm the Reduction
of Naples - some English men
the Vice Roy of sicillys Galleys
as he was goeing to his Goverment
and himself is Escaped with Another
