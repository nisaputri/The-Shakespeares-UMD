Sunday Last the Royall Sovereigne Royall Catherine
Dunkirk - yesterday her majesty took
a view of the Presents sent her by the Empero<sup>r</sup>
of Fesse and Morroccho _This day
Doctor Brown was tryed at Guild hall
for writeing of the Pamphlett entitled
the Country Parsons letter to the Lord
Keeper and found guilty of the same_
The Court is to passe sentence
upon him next Terme
This day at the Queenes Bench
was argued Doctor Drakes (Not for Not)
in his Indictment which after full
deferred their Judgment till a
further consideration
