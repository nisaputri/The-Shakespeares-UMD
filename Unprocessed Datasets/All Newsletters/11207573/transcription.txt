S<ex>i</ex><sl>r</sl> London March 31<sup>st</sup>. 1709
Hague Aprill the 5<sup>th</sup>. The Garryson
in Namure being Reduced to Want
some ffrench soldiers on Tuesday
last plundered 2 Bakers Shops and
took away their Bread telling them
that the King owed them Money
and it was better to plunder
than starve, In Tournay, Cambray
Disturbances on the same Account
It is Confidently Discourst here
that at the last Conferrence of
the 3 Deputyes of the States with
Mons<ex>ieu</ex><sup>r</sup>. Roville at Woerden all
matters Relateing to a Generall
peace were as good as Settled, In
which the ffrench King not only
gives up Spain and the West Indies
to King Charles (being Contented with
a pension for the Duke of Anjou)
