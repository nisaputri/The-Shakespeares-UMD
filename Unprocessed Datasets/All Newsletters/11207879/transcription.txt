London Oct 11th 1709
Count Gallas the Emperors Envoy here
has Signifyed to Our Ministry that
from Barcelona that Count Guido of
Starembergh not only made 3
Regiments Prisoners of Warr but
fell on the Rear of the Spanish
Army and cut off 1500 Men - Wee
are Informed that Brigadeer Genll.
Macknarly is Restored to favour
and will shortly be Imployed on
some great Occassion - His Excellency
Dayes for Holland has Orders to goe
directly to the Camp of the Czar of
Muscovy and to the Utmost of
his Power to make a peace amongst
