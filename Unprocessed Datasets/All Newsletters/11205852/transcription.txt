Edinburgh July the 8<sup>th</sup> the privy Councell
Edinburgh July the 8<sup>th</sup>. The privy Councill Sate
yesterday and had before them one Gordon
a Jesuitt who lately came from Saint
Germans. his Lodging were Searched
and Severall Commissions were found
under the name of the pretended
Edinburgh Castle in order to be further
of Queensborough and conduct him
