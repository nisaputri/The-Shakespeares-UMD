Last tuesday night two ffoot
padds had the impudence for to
assault a Gentleman near
Whitehall - who drew his sword &
defended himselfe till the Centinells
from the guardrooms roome came to
his assistance, and knockt them
down beateing them in a cruell
manner soe that it is beleived one
of them will die of his wounds:
they were carryed before a
Justice of peace who committed
them to Newgate -- a Privateere
