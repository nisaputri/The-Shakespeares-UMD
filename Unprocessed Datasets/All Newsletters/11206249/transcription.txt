And Quarantini, and two hundred
Gentlemen all of that Dutchy, and
brought them prisoners to the Castle
which occasioned some disturbance
and it is said that a design is on foot
to seize the Cittadell, and free those
Noblemen & declare for King Charles
the third - on satterday last some ffrench
officers went to Southampton to receive
two hundred Dragoons, of Coll Carpenters
regiment forthwith to compleate the
ffrench regiment of Dragoons raiseing
here who are to goe on board the ffleet
for the Descent - It is said that the
ffrench have taken the Bonnetta Sloop
that was cruizeing lately near Dunkirk -
yesterday about 30 compleate Gunnery
